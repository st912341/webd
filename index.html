Frequently Asked Questions

What is an explainer video?
An explainer video is a short, promotional video that explains a company's product or service in a clear, engaging way. These videos are often hosted on landing pages or featured on company websites, and may also be used to advertise products and services on social media. Explainer videos are a useful tool for businesses to introduce their products or services to potential customers and explain how they can benefit from them.
How much does an explainer video cost?
The cost of an explainer video can vary depending on a number of factors, including the length of the video, the complexity of the animation, the voiceover talent, and the production company. However, you can generally expect to pay anywhere from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars for a professional explainer video.
What are the benefits of using an explainer video?
There are many benefits to using an explainer video, including:
  • Increased engagement and understanding
  • Improved brand awareness
  • Higher conversion rates
  • Enhanced customer satisfaction
  • Greater reach and visibility
